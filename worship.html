Confession of Sin

Lord, in your mercy hear our prayer. We confess that it is still all too easy for us to sacrifice our convictions for convenience, your standards for status, your principles for promotion, your absolutes for our ambition, our souls for shallow and unsatisfying success. How easily we are seduced by power, prestige, pleasure, or possessions— seduced into violating our integrity or harming our fellowship with you. From earth’s fullest bliss we turn to you again, unfulfilled. Forgive us our half-hearted devotion and our double-minded spirit. In the name of Christ who gave himself to save us we pray. Amen.

Bangladesh, Week 4: Missions 98.8% of the Bangladeshi population of 162 million people remain unreached with the gospel, meaning that in 98.8% of the population there is no indigenous community of believing Christians able to evangelize to their own people groups. 2,683 missionaries are needed to effectively communicate the gospel to Bangladeshi people so that they can in turn effectively reach their own communities and people. This is difficult, more missionaries have been increasingly forced out, and the environment is growing in hostility, but still there has been growth of believers. The Kayastha people in Bangladesh are 100% Hindu, there is no known church movement within these people who make up the 4th largest population in Bangladesh. Many public, elite figures in politics, education, and other areas of society come from this people group.
  • Pray for God to send out laborers into the harvest, for our church to be burdened in prayer for Bangladesh, and for us to be sent out to those unreached with the saving news of Jesus Christ.
  • Pray for Kayastha Bangladeshi people to have open hearts to hear and receive the gospel, and pray for messengers of the gospel so that they would be able to hear.

Invitation to the Lord's Supper

This Supper proclaims the death of our Lord Jesus until he comes again in glory, when he will wipe away every tear from our eyes, and death shall be no more. It points to the Marriage Supper of the Lamb, where we shall be the bride of Christ--all those who have washed their robes and made them white in the blood of the Lamb.
